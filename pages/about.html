Our Story

The charm and playfulness of the design and the imaginative use of scale engage local and international visitors. They can also access a café and a boutique lounge, where they can browse the bookshelves stocked with an extensive collection of magazines published from May 1975 until today, offering a chance to rediscover 50 years of fashion, art, and culture. During this anniversary year, the Juan Flórez store becomes not only a celebration of the brand but also a tribute to the city’s architectural heritage and its history.

Enjoy Watching

On May 9, 1975, Amancio Ortega opened the first Zara store in A Coruña, Spain. At the time, his company, Confecciones GOA, had been producing garments for a little over a decade, since 1963. The opening of this first Zara establishment marked a pivotal moment for a company that has been making and distributing clothing ever since. Under the Zara brand, the company emerged as a fashion retailer with Women’s, Men’s, and Kids’ collections and gradually built and strengthened a philosophy of placing its customers at the heart of its decision-making, listening attentively to their preferences, and offering them quality fashion at affordable prices.
